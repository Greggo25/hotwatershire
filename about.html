Our Story

Hot Water Experts Serving All of Sydney

Hot Water Sydney was founded with a simple mission: give Sydney homeowners a reliable, honest hot water service they can count on. We got tired of hearing about plumbers who showed up late, quoted one price and charged another, or recommended expensive replacements when a simple repair would do.

Our team of licensed NSW plumbers specialises exclusively in hot water systems — it's all we do, and we do it very well. That focus means we diagnose problems faster, carry the right parts more often, and give you honest advice about whether to repair or replace.

We've helped hundreds of Sydney households get their hot water restored — often the same day they call. From Bondi to Penrith, Hornsby to Cronulla, we cover all of greater Sydney.
