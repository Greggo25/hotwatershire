Answers to the most common questions we get from Sutherland Shire homeowners about hot water systems.

We service all major brands including Rheem, Rinnai, Dux, Bosch, Chromagen, Vulcan, Everhot, AquaMAX, Thermann, Stiebel Eltron, Saxon, Quantum, Apricus and Wilson Solar. If you have a brand not listed, call us — we service them all.

Electric and gas storage systems typically last 8–12 years. Continuous flow systems last 15–20 years. Solar and heat pump systems can last 15–20+ years with regular servicing.

Yes. NSW homeowners upgrading to an energy-efficient hot water system (heat pump or solar) may be eligible for rebates under the NSW Energy Savings Scheme and federal STC rebates. These can reduce the cost of a new system by $300–$1,000+. Ask us about your eligibility when you call.

If your system is under 8 years old, repair is usually cost-effective. If it's over 10–12 years old and showing problems, replacement often makes more economic sense, especially with energy-efficient options and available rebates. Our plumbers will give you an honest assessment.
